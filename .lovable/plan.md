

# Nepal Contractor Bidding Document Assistant

A personal tool to help you quickly prepare and manage bidding documents for Nepal's public procurement system (PPMO), with no login required — all data stored in your browser.

## Pages & Features

### 1. Dashboard
- Overview of active bids with deadlines and completion status
- Quick-start buttons to create a new bid for each type (NCB Single Envelope, NCB Two Envelope, Sealed Quotation, ICB)

### 2. My Company Profile (one-time setup)
- Save your recurring company details: Company name, address, PAN/VAT number, registration number, authorized representative, contact info
- Upload/store reusable documents: Company registration certificate, VAT/PAN certificate, tax clearance, audited financial statements, bank guarantee templates

### 3. New Bid Preparation (core feature)
Select bid type and get a **dynamic checklist + fillable templates** for each:

**Single Envelope (NCB) — all documents in one envelope:**
- ✅ Letter of Bid (fillable template)
- ✅ Bill of Quantities / Activity Schedule (fillable template)
- ✅ Bid Security (bank guarantee template)
- ✅ Qualification Information: general experience, specific experience, financial capacity, personnel, equipment
- ✅ Company registration & VAT/PAN certificates
- ✅ Tax clearance certificate
- ✅ Audited financial statements
- ✅ Power of Attorney / authorization letter
- ✅ JV Agreement (if applicable)
- ✅ Technical proposal: methodology, work schedule, organization chart

**Double Envelope (NCB) — separate Technical & Financial envelopes:**

*Envelope 1 (Technical):*
- ✅ Letter of Technical Bid (template)
- ✅ Qualification Information (experience, capacity, personnel, equipment)
- ✅ Company registration & tax documents
- ✅ Bid Security
- ✅ Technical proposal: methodology, work plan, schedule, org chart
- ✅ Power of Attorney

*Envelope 2 (Financial):*
- ✅ Letter of Financial Bid (template)
- ✅ Bill of Quantities / Priced Activity Schedule
- ✅ Summary of costs

**Sealed Quotation:**
- ✅ Quotation letter (template)
- ✅ Priced Bill of Quantities
- ✅ Company registration
- ✅ VAT/PAN certificate
- ✅ Tax clearance

**ICB Works:**
- Same as NCB Two Envelope plus additional international requirements (eligibility declarations, country of origin forms, currency requirements)

### 4. Document Templates
- Pre-filled Letter of Bid with your company info — just edit project-specific details (project name, employer, amount, dates)
- Pre-filled Qualification Information forms
- Bank Guarantee template for bid security
- Power of Attorney template
- BOQ spreadsheet-style entry form

### 5. Bid Tracker
- List of all your bids with: project name, employer, bid type, submission deadline, status (Preparing / Submitted / Won / Lost)
- Visual progress bar showing how many documents are ready vs pending
- Deadline reminders highlighted in red when approaching

### 6. Stored Documents Library
- Upload and organize your reusable documents (certificates, financial statements, etc.)
- Tag documents by type for quick retrieval during bid preparation

## Design
- Clean, professional interface with a Nepal government / construction feel
- Nepali and English labels where relevant
- Card-based layout with clear progress indicators
- All data persisted in browser localStorage (no backend needed)
